Receipt Keeper is a website that will allow users to upload their receipts (from supermarkets, big box stores, restaurants, etc.), keep track of their purchases and favorites, and obtain discounts and offers from partner vendors. Users may submit photos of their receipts or enter data manually. In the future, integration with online vendors may be possible.

Often, receipts will only show a partial or abbreviated line for each item being sold - for example, "EFF PCN NTCKE CKIE" for Effie's Pecan Nutcakes. Receipt Keeper will determine the best possible match for each item.
